About Me

About Me – Mansoureh (Mansi)

Hi, I’m Mansi—a passionate and certified beauty professional with over a decade of experience in the industry, both internationally and here in New Zealand. With qualifications in beauty therapy and makeup artistry, I specialize in creating tailored, flawless looks that bring out your natural beauty. Whether it's bridal makeup, soft glam, or bold transformations, I take pride in customizing every look to suit your features, skin tone, and personal style. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working behind the scenes at events like the Pacific Fusion Fashion Show, VIP runway shows, and the Miss New Zealand Pageant, where I’ve gained a reputation for professionalism, attention to detail, and creative versatility. I’m also certified in a range of advanced beauty services, including: – Lash Extensions & Lash Lifts – Brow Lamination & Microblading – Lip Blush (semi-permanent lip tint) My goal is simple: to help every client feel confident, polished, and empowered—because beauty isn’t just about how you look, but how you feel.
